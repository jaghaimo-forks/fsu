Location: at Providence outer system jump point
Date: 206.4.3

The sensors have picked up a hostile fleet with-in interception range, On closer inspection it appears to be a Union resupply mission on its way to Citadel Providence. The Enemy Fleet is well defended it's clearly a mission of higher importance. After some consideration Captain Wayne decides to move into intercept. An Ambitous move, But a clean victory will surely earn him some serious recognition in the Church.
